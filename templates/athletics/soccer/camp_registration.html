HOW TO REGISTER: Complete this form and submit it by clicking on the "Register" button.

Method of Payment

Note: The Carthage web site supports secure credit card transactions.

Credit Cards are only accepted through online registration, not at check-ins. If you have a balance coming or did not pay in full at time of registration, cash or check are the only methods of payment.

After you complete the registration form, we will send confirmation via email.
