Time Slots

There are two audition sessions from which to choose a 10 minute slot:

  • Tuesday, September 3: 9:00am—8:00pm

Freshman and Transfer Students

Make certain that you check your orientation schedule.

Returning Students

You are encouraged to sign up Monday 2-4pm or Tuesday 9-12pm.

Choral Auditions consist of:

  1. Vocalizations
  2. Tonal Memory Exercises
  3. Sigh Reading
  4. Singing "My Country 'Tis of Thee" in different keys

A prepared song is not required, but you are welcome to perform it.
