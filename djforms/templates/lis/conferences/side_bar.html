Conference Housing

Conference housing is in the Oaks Village, a single-person housing complex comprised of six buildings. Each room has its own bathroom and is air-conditioned. Each floor features a shared living room with a couch, chairs, television, study tables and a kitchenette with a sink, microwave, and refrigerator. The Oaks sits in a wooded area on the shores of Lake Michigan.
